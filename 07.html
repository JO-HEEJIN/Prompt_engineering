Zero-Shot Prompting vs Chain of Thought Prompting

ID Aspect Zero-Shot Prompting Chain of Thought Prompting
1 Definition

  • Zero-Shot Prompting
    • Generation of output without training data.

    •

  • Chain of Thought Prompting
    • Guidance through a step-by-step reasoning process to arrive at the answer.

    •
2 Usage

  • Use for tasks that require immediate response with minimal input.

    •

  • Use for tasks that require logical reasoning and problem-solving.

    •
3 Advantage

  • Efficiency in simple tasks without need for deep understanding.

    •

  • Improvement in accuracy and transparency in reasoning processes for complex tasks.

    •
4 Example

  • Generation of text completion with minimal input.

    •

  • Solving of math problems or puzzles step-by-step.

    •
